Eco Buddy App

Environmentally Conscious Options Mobile App Case Study

THE PROBLEM:

Recycling and properly disposing of waste is a time-consuming and difficult endeavor even for the most environmentally conscious individuals. Additionally, researching sustainable products frequently results in unattainable aspirations rather than actual purchasing practices.

PROJECT TEAM:

Christine Her, Camden Hirshfeld, and Liz Lake

THE SOLUTION:

A mobile app that helps users make more sustainable purchases when shopping and helps them recycle the items they purchase more efficiently and effectively.

TOOLS:

Google Suite, Miro, Figma

User & Market Research

Objective

As user researchers, we want to understand the challenges individuals face when they try to recycle or make environmentally-conscious purchases.

Methodology

User Survey: We conducted a 12-question Google Forms survey about usersâ€™ recycling and purchasing habits via email and social media. There were 33 respondents ranging in age from 18-70+.

User Interviews: 5 user interviews were conducted to collect qualitative data from participants about their recycling and purchasing habits. We interviewed 5 individuals, ranging in age from 21-76. User interviews ranged in length from 10 to 25 minutes.

Market / Competitor Analysis: 4 competitors within the recycling mobile app market were analyzed to find what strengths, weaknesses and opportunities in the market exist.
